Limitations & Disclaimers

Understanding the boundaries and disclaimers of Pixel Punch

Benchmark Limitations

Please be aware of the following limitations when using Pixel Punch:

  • All results represent estimated benchmarks derived from computational models
  • Actual performance may vary based on driver versions, system configuration, background processes, and game settings
  • Results are based on theoretical calculations and may not reflect real-world performance
  • GPU and CPU performance estimates are approximations
  • External factors like temperature, power management, and system load can affect actual performance

Disclaimers

Important disclaimers about using this tool:

  • This tool supports a limited set of hardware configurations:
    • CPU:
        Intel i3, i5, i7, i9
        AMD Ryzen 4000, 5000, 7000, 9000 Series
    • RAM: 4, 8, 16, 32, 64GB
    • GPU: 1, 2, 4, 8, 12, 16 GB VRAM
Results for systems outside these specifications may be inaccurate
  • This tool is intended for educational and analytical purposes only
  • Results should not be considered definitive performance guarantees
  • We are not responsible for any hardware purchasing decisions based on our results
  • Real-world testing is recommended for critical hardware decisions
  • Performance can vary significantly between different game titles and settings

    • Recommendations

    For best results and accurate assessments:

    • Use our benchmarks as a starting point, not the final decision factor
    • Check manufacturer specifications and independent reviews
    • Consider your specific use cases and gaming preferences
    • Test with your actual games and workloads when possible
    • Monitor real-time performance metrics during gameplay
